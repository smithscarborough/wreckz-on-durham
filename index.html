A dedication to the countless automobile accidents that occur at or near the intersection of Durham Dr & Center St.

Note: We sincerely hope that no one was injured in any of these accidents. The purpose of this site is simply to highlight the now ridiculous number of accidents that happen at this particular intersection in Houston, with the hope that the additional attention might bring about change.
